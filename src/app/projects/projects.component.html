SkillAmp

Call Center Software that helps reduce agent attrition rates.

Our software surveys customers who spoke with call center agents and provides detailed feedback on what customers like, dislike, and recommend for your agents. Twitter Bot

I have noticed that there are lots of contests on twitter that require you to like, retweet, and follow the person in order to enter the contest. I decided to take advantage of this and increase my chances of winning by creating a Twitter Bot using the Twitter API. This bot was built with NodeJS and enters up to 500 contests per day automatically.
